About Me

A brief introduction about myself including my academic background and career.

July 24, 1998

I have completed my Bacheors Degree in Statistics, Post Graduate Diploma in Banking and Relationship Management and currently in my last year Masters degree viz MBA in Data Analytics and Data Science.

Throughout my education and professional journey, I have honed my analytical skills, primarily through practical experience in programming languages and data tools. My proficiency in SQL, Python, Tableau, and Power BI has allowed me to work on several impactful projects. For instance, I recently completed a coronavirus data analysis project, where I segregated elements to uncover infection rates and vaccine effectiveness.

I have also developed a dashboard on Airbnb price listings using Tableau, providing deep insights into market trends for specific geographical areas. I have also completed data cleaning and visualization projects, such as creating a dashboard using Power BI based on data from surveys of data professionals, showcasing various insights and trends in the industry. My familiarity with Advanced Excel, including pivot tables, data cleaning, and the creation of MIS presentations, further supports my technical skills in data processing.

My current goal is to explore opportunities where i can apply my data analytical skills to drive strategic decision-making advances and support business growth.
